Frequently Asked Questions

What courses does VK Academy offer?

VK Academy offers coaching for students from Class 9 to postgraduate level, including Class 9–10, Class 11–12 (Science & Commerce), undergraduate, postgraduate, and B.Tech courses like Engineering Mathematics.

Are demo classes available before enrollment?

Yes, we provide 5 days of free demo classes so students can experience our teaching style and interact with our expert faculty before making a decision.

How do i know the right course for me?

Our academic counselors will assess your current grade, career goals, and subject strengths to recommend the most suitable course . You can also attend demo classes to explore your interests before deciding.

Do you offer online or offline classes?

VK Academy offers both offline classroom coaching and online sessions, depending on student preferences and availability.

Are there regular tests and progress tracking?

Yes, we conduct weekly tests, doubt-clearing sessions, and monthly performance reports to monitor each student’s academic progress.

What are the class timings at VK Academy?

We offer flexible timings including morning, afternoon, and evening batches. You can choose a time slot that best fits your school/college schedule.

Are study materials provided?

Yes, VK Academy provides well-structured study materials, notes, and practice worksheets for every course, curated by subject experts.

Is there any entrance test for joining VK Academy?

No entrance test is required for most courses. However, certain advanced batches may have an assessment to place you in the right level.

Do you offer personalized doubt-clearing sessions?

Yes, we conduct regular one-on-one doubt-clearing sessions to ensure every student fully understands the concepts taught in class.

Can I switch batches if I miss classes or need a different time?

Yes, based on availability, you can switch to another batch. We also offer recorded sessions and revision classes to help you catch up.
